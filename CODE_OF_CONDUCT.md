# Code of Conduct

Amulet Laboratories is committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all members.

## Our Standards

- **Respect**: Treat all members with dignity and professionalism
- **Inclusion**: Welcome diverse perspectives and backgrounds
- **Collaboration**: Work constructively toward shared goals
- **Accountability**: Take responsibility for your actions and words
- **Excellence**: Strive for quality in all work and interactions

## Expected Behavior

- Communicate clearly and kindly
- Give and receive constructive feedback gracefully
- Acknowledge and respect others' expertise and contributions
- Contribute to a healthy, professional environment

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hostile conduct
- Personal attacks or demeaning language
- Disrespect toward colleagues' work or ideas
- Violation of privacy or confidentiality
- Any behavior that undermines team trust or safety

## Enforcement

Violations should be reported to the organization leadership. All reports will be handled confidentially and investigated promptly.

---

This code of conduct applies to all Amulet Laboratories spaces: repositories, discussions, commits, issues, and external communications on behalf of the organization.
